Recycle Catcher Game

Game Rules

  • You're driving a truck to collect Recyclables.
  • Each time you collect a recyclable item, you'll increase your score. Each time you miss one, you'll lose one life. The game is lost once you run out of lives
  • Some items should not go in the recyclable bin! Don't collect them or you'll lose more lives.
  • The higher your score gets, the quicker the items fall ! Sharpen your reflexes if you want to become a sorting hero!
